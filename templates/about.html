About us

I am Deep chhetri grew up in siliguri, foothills of Himalaya. I own and operates a commercial photo processing studio that was featured in magazines, Ads and other news outlets, describing many extraordinary events, people and places, as an amateur photographer The transition to photojournalistic wedding photography was seamless, thanks to a natural curiosity and ability to connect to his subjects to bring out the best emotion, angle and proper lighting. At weddings, he says, "I coordinate with my staff to help capture personalities and relationship dynamics without exerting influence on the proceedings." Deep lives with his family in the beautiful mountainside of Darjeeling, India

Our team consists of highly qualified professionals and above all they love what they do "we are a master in sophisticated software and other tools in pre and post production, to guarantee end results that will surprise and delight. That is why we love what we do; it makes people happy. Shooting style is comprehensive, utilizing the latest technology. "Every photo tells a story – this is a real story of one of the most important days in a couple’s life; and it must be documented with skill, caring and experience." At a wedding, we are in a very intimate setting where emotions run high. We're very easy; we're not directing people or trying to run the show, and so we're a calming influence on the couple, their attendants, and the guests.
